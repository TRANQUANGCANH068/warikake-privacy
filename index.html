Privacy Policy for Warikake

Last updated: June 16, 2026

```

Warikake is a mobile application designed to help groups record shared expenses and calculate how much each member should pay or receive during trips or group activities. This Privacy Policy explains how Warikake handles user data.

1. Information We Collect

Warikake may store the following information when you use the app:

  • Trip or group names
  • Member nicknames
  • Expense descriptions
  • Expense amounts
  • The member who paid an expense
  • The members included in expense splitting
  • Group IDs or invitation codes used to join trips
  • Local trip history stored on your device

2. How We Use Information

The information is used only to provide the main features of the app, including:

  • Creating and joining shared trips
  • Recording group expenses
  • Calculating balances between members
  • Displaying trip history on the same device
  • Sharing trip access through QR codes or group IDs

3. Data Storage

Warikake may store data in Firebase services, such as Cloud Firestore, to allow group members to access shared trip and expense information. Warikake may also store local trip history on your device using local storage.

4. Data Sharing

Warikake does not sell your personal data. Trip and expense data may be visible to people who have access to the same group ID or QR code. Please share group IDs and QR codes only with people you trust.

5. Local Data

Some information, such as your recently opened trips, may be stored locally on your device. You can clear this local trip history from the app settings. Clearing local history does not delete the shared trip data stored in Firebase.

6. Third-Party Services

Warikake may use Firebase services provided by Google to store and manage app data. Firebase may process data according to Google's own privacy and security practices.

7. Camera and Photos

If receipt photo features are added in the future, Warikake may request access to your camera or photo library. These permissions will be used only for features related to attaching receipt images to expenses.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect app data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure. Users should avoid sharing sensitive personal or financial information inside trip names, nicknames, or expense descriptions.

9. Children's Privacy

Warikake is not intended for children under the age required by applicable laws. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we update it, we will change the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
```
